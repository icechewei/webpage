Notes

    Traveling is like a poem. I can not increase the length of it, but I can use my footprints to fill each word, include my experiences to strengthen every sentence, and listen to my emotions to tell the whole story. I have been to United States, China, Belgium, Germany, Switzerland, Austria, Macau, Denmark, Sweden, Estonia, Finland, Japan, and New Zealand. Hope that I can see one of the Eight-thousanders someday.
